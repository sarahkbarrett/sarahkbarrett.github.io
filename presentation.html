HTML Headings

Why are Headings Important?

Headings are an important part of typography in webpages. They help you establish heirarchy and helps the reader understand what they will be reading on your page. Headings inform readers of new topics and ideas in your content.

What are the Different Headings?

  • H1: bold, very large font, typically used as major headings or titles
  • H2: bold, large font, typically subtitles
  • H3: large, italicized font, slightly indented
  • H4: bold, normal font, indented more than H3
  • H5: italicized, normal font, indented as H4
  • H6: bold, normal font, indented same as normal font
