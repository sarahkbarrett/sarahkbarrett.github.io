Site Plan

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to inform people of the current weather conditions and the weather conditions in the near future.

Audience Description

The audience of this website will be anyone between the ages of 15 and 70 that has access to a computer or a mobile device. The website will draw a very wide range of people. These people will want to access the weather conditions to know what to wear for the day or two plan for travel to work or school. The audience will be using anything from their phones to a desktop computer to view this website, with either high-speed internet or dial-up. They will be looking for the current temperature and the weather conditions for the near future. The website will generally be visited in the mornings when the audience is planning their day, outfits and travel. They'll be in their homes or at work, preparing to leave the comfort of their home and go outside.

Persona

Demographics:

He also takes his cats for walks each day, so he likes to know if the weather is nice enough to take them outside or if he should take them around his indoor track in his 2,000 sq. foot basement.

Environment: Joe is very tech savvy. He has a smart house and likes that he can talk to his technology. He spends a lot of time on Twitter and trying to remember his old Myspace password. Using a weather app is very easy for him.
